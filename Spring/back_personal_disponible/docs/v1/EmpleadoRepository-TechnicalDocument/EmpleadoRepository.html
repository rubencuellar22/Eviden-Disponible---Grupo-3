The methods include:

- findByBench: Finds employees by their bench date.
- findByJornada: Finds employees by their working hours.
- findByCiudad: Finds employees by their city.
- findbyN4: Finds employees by their N4.
- findbyCategoria: Finds employees by their category.
- findbyScr: Finds employees by their SCR (Score) between 1 and 100.
- findByGroups: Finds employees by their group name.

These methods return a list of employees that match the specified criteria.
